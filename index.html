SEC Football Stadiums

University of Kentucky Mapping 671

The map presents all 14 SEC football stadiums with the stadium's name. When the map is zoomed in the icon sizes will changed based on the stadiums capacity. A link to the individual stadium map points can be found below, along with the geojson link and tools used in making this map. The goal of this map is to provided the location of the university stadiums on one interactive map. Furthermore, the capacity of each stadium can be compared with the location of the stadium and can show the rural areas in which the stadiums are located.

The Southeastern Conference is know for being the best college football conference in the US. The conference holds universities with the best facilities despite most of the conference being located in rural areas. Historically the SEC annually has the highest profitability and sends the most players to the NFL compared to other conferences.

Tools Used in this Project

  • Data is shown on Mapbox web map with geolocation services enabled.
  • GDAL and QGIS 3.18.2 to convert raster the national dataset. Capacity and University name was added in Mapbox
  • Data for capacity from Saturady Down South from 2019
